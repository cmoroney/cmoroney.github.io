About Me

Chicago marketer turned Austin statistician

As a seasoned data analyst with a strong foundation in marketing, I leverage my diverse skill set to uncover actionable insights and fuel business growth. A Chicago native and DePaul University graduate, I initially honed my expertise in digital marketing, managing AdWords campaigns and optimizing SEO strategies. Recognizing the transformative power of data, I pursued advanced studies in statistics, earning an M.S. from Texas A&M University in 2022. My ongoing commitment to professional development ensures I remain at the forefront of data science methodologies, enabling me to deliver impactful results in today's data-driven landscape.
